About

Bio

Ben Don Sherwood is a character-focused concept artist and story artist with 20+ years across traditional 2D animation, 3D development, and storyboard-driven visual narrative. A former in-betweener at Disney’s Sydney studio, he brings old-school craftsmanship and contemporary digital fluency to create work anchored in character, heart, and timing.

  • Strengths: character performance, storyboarding & animatics, color scripting, cross-discipline collaboration
  • Tools: Photoshop, Storyboard Pro, Procreate, Maya, Blender, After Effects, Premiere Pro
